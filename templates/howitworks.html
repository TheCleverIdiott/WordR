How FlipAnalyse Works

Social Media

Social Media is very important to understand how customers perceive any brand. Thus metadata including posts & tweets collected from various social media platforms like - LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit act as a great source of analyzing the sentiments of customer satisfaction regarding Flipkart. Thus we have extracted top 100 tweets and posts from each of the social media platforms to perform overall sentiment analysis of posts regarding Flipkart, or tagged #Flipkart.

Top Products

Electronics play a major role in Flipkart's sales, and thus we have come up with a solution to analyze these sales by extracting top items under multiple product categories in the Electronics section including Laptops, Monitors, Mobile Phones, Headphones etc. FlipAnalyze will pave a way to create meaningful business decisions such as dictating brand deals and organizing targeted sales related to certain products.

Big Billion Days Sale

Big Billion Days is the largest Ecommerce Sale organized by Flipkart. Most Flipkart users aim to buy as many products as possible during this sale, thus making an analysis of these products extremely vital in boosting Flipkart's sales and revenue. FlipAnalyse provides a detailed analysis of customer feedback and product details from the sale to display information about trending items and customer reviews.
